⚜ How to Play ⚜

👋 Welcome, Collector

You're here to collect cards. All 1,201 of them. Plus the variants. Plus the things the catalogue wasn't built to contain. It'll take a while. That's the point.

💰 The Loop

Hit Forge Coin to make £5 at a time. Save up £100 and hit Open Pack. Five cards come out face-down — tap each one to flip it. When you're done, hit Sell & Log All.

Lower-tier cards sell for cash. Rarer cards sell for gems. Gems are how the game actually progresses — more on those in a moment.

💡 Upgrade Idle Flow early. It earns gold while you're away. Level 5 pays for itself within minutes.

📖 The Grand Binder

Every card you find gets logged here. Use the tabs to filter by tier. Search works too. The gaps are as important as the cards — they're where the wanting lives.

💎 Gems

Uncommon cards and above sell for gems instead of gold. Three matter immediately:

💎Blue — boosts your Rare+ drop rates. Stack as many as you can.
🔴Red — collect 5 and you automatically unlock a random missing card.
🟣Purple — collect 5 and your pack size grows by 1 (up to 20 cards per pack).

Other gem types will reveal themselves when the time is right.

🃏 Cards & Variants

There are 1,201 base cards across 9 tiers. Each one can appear in multiple variants — Gilded, Rainbow, Damaged, and others you'll encounter as you play. Some variants are crafted. Some are found. Some are generated by something the catalogue was not designed to handle.

💡 The Binder tabs filter by tier. The search bar works on names and rarity.

🔓 The Rest of It

New features unlock as you open packs. When something arrives, the game will tell you what it does. You don't need to know about the Forge before the Forge exists.

What's worth knowing now: the game auto-saves every 60 seconds. Your progress is safe. This button — ✉️ — is always in the top-right corner if you want to come back to this guide.

🏆 Achievements & The Chronicle

The Chronicle logs everything that happens. Achievements track milestones — they pop up when you hit them. Neither requires your attention. Both reward it.

🎵 One More Thing

There are hidden cards in the game that are not in the catalogue. There are things the Binder can show you that have no official name. There is a room behind the Exchange that is not on any floor plan.

The game does not announce these. It simply waits.

Good luck, Collector. ⚜
